Baluku Josephats - Portfolio Website Plan

1. Site Name

Site Name: www.baluku.me

This name reflects my personal brand as a software developer. It’s simple, professional, and unique—helping me build an online presence and stand out in tech spaces.

2. Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to serve as a personal portfolio to showcase my skills, projects, and experiences as a software developer. It will include information about me, project case studies, a downloadable CV, and a contact form for potential clients or employers.

3. Scenarios

  • Where can I download Baluku’s CV and portfolio?
  • How can I get in touch with Baluku for a freelance or job opportunity?

4. Color Schema

  • Pink (#ff69b4) – Used for all main headings and borders.
  • Purple (#a020f0) –.Used for buttons, links, and accents.
  • Light Green (#e0ffe0) – Used for background of content sections.

5. Typography

  • Poppins – Used for all body text and paragraphs.
  • Segoe UI – Used for headings and emphasized sections.
