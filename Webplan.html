Website Planning Document

Site Name

Creative Youth Network - This name reflects the mission of empowering young people through creativity and innovation.

Site Purpose

The site serves as a platform for youth to learn new creative skills, find resources for their talents, and connect with others in the creative industry.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: A young artist wants to learn more about film production.
  • Scenario 2: A parent wants to find workshops that can help their child with dance.

Color Schema

Primary Color: #FF5733 (Used for headings and buttons)

Secondary Color: #C70039 (Used for accents and links)

Typography

Font: Roboto (Used for body text and navigation links)
